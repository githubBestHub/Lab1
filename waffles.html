I really like Waffles

Here is a good Waffle recipe (stolen from allrecipes.com, I cannot confirm nor deny the deliciosity of the recipe)

  1. Preheat a Waffle Iron
  2. Whisk 2 large eggs in a bowl
  3. Add 2 cups all-purpose flour, 1.75 cups milk, .5 cup vegetable oil, and mix
  4. Add 1 tablespoon white sugar
  5. Whisk in 4 teaspoons baking powder, .25 teaspoon salt, 0.5 teaspoon vanilla extract
  6. Whisk until smooth, but do not overmix
  7. Spray waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray
  8. Cook the waffles and enjoy!
